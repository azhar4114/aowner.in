What to keep ready

  • Owner or authorized decision-maker details.
  • Expected price, revenue, profit, and rent bands.
  • At least one public proof link: Google Maps, Instagram, or Website.
  • A business summary of at least 150 characters and a sale reason of at least 25 characters.

What happens next
